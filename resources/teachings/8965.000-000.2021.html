The training activities of your choice will be scheduled in the academic year 2023/24.

4. Semester Buenos Aires

The semester abroad could be attended by a restricted number of students enrolled who obtained at least 65 CFU credits, selected by the criteria listed in the related call for application. Criteria are the following: - Weighted average (75%) - Motivation letter (15%) - Certification of Spanish Language (10%)
